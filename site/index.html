Frequently asked questions

Everything about capturing, annotating and sharing with RvgeShot.

Is RvgeShot free?

Yes. RvgeShot is completely free, needs no account, and works on both Windows and macOS.

How do I take a screenshot with RvgeShot?

Press your Print Screen hotkey, drag to select any region of the screen, annotate it if you like, then copy, save locally, or share it via a private link.

Does RvgeShot work on both Windows and Mac?

Yes. RvgeShot runs on Windows 10/11 (64-bit) and on macOS 12 or newer, on both Apple Silicon and Intel.

What makes RvgeShot more private than other screenshot tools?

Share links use ~128-bit entropy so nobody can guess them, you can add a password and an expiry of 1 hour, 1 day, 7 days or never, and uploads are never indexed by search engines.
